quantlib.models.shortrate.onefactormodels.hullwhite.HullWhite¶

class HullWhite(YieldTermStructure term_structure=YieldTermStructure(), Real a=0, Real sigma=0)¶

Bases: quantlib.models.shortrate.onefactormodels.vasicek.Vasicek

Single-factor Hull-White (extended Vasicek) model. The standard single-factor Hull-White model is defined by .. math:: dr_t = ( heta(t) - lpha r_t)dt + sigma dW_t

where lpha and sigma are constants.
