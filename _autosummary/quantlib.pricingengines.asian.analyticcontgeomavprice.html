AnalyticContinuousGeometricAveragePriceAsianEngine(...)

Pricing engine for European continuous geometric average price Asian This class implements a continuous geometric average price Asian option with European exercise. The formula is from "Option Pricing Formulas", E. G. Haug (1997) pag 96-97. ingroup asianengines test - the correctness of the returned value is tested by reproducing results available in literature, and results obtained using a discrete average approximation. - the correctness of the returned greeks is tested by reproducing numerical derivatives.
